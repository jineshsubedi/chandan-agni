Who We Are

Three Decades of Driving Nepal Forward

Agni Group began in 1991 as the exclusive authorized distributor of Mahindra utility vehicles and tractors in Nepal. Over three decades, we've grown into a nationwide mobility conglomerate — serving hundreds of thousands of Nepali families.

Our mission is simple: empower people, enrich lives, and contribute to Nepal's development through accessible, reliable mobility solutions.

To The Top Of The World

Mahindra Spirit

Popular Nepali actress Nisha Adhikari has become the first Nepali actress to climb the world’s highest peak – Mt. Everest. She successfully climbed Mt. Everest on Tuesday (21/05/2013) morning at 8:00 am.

By summiting Everest and Lhotse within 48 hours, she’s turned ambition into altitude — a true embodiment of the Mahindra spirit: fearless, determined, and built for challenges.

"From our logo to her legacy — this moment is mountain-sized."
