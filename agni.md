## Executive Summary

Agni Group is one of Nepal's leading automotive-focused business groups. Founded in 1991, it began as the authorized distributor of Mahindra utility vehicles and tractors in Nepal and has since grown into a diversified group with interests in automobile sales, after-sales services, energy solutions, logistics, two-wheelers, and related sectors. The group is best known for representing the Mahindra brand in Nepal. ([agnigroup.com.np][1])

## History and Background

Agni Group started operations in 1991 as the authorized distributor of Mahindra utility vehicles and tractors. Over more than three decades, it has expanded from a vehicle importer into a nationwide automotive and mobility business with extensive sales and service infrastructure. ([agnigroup.com.np][2])

Key milestones include:

* 1991: Began as Mahindra's authorized distributor in Nepal.
* 2022: Celebrated 30 years of operations.
* 2023: Launched Mahindra XUV300 and XUV700 in Nepal.
* 2025: Announced plans to launch Mahindra's electric SUVs (BE6 and XEV9e) in Nepal. ([agnigroup.com.np][3])
