About Us

Welcome to Agni Group

Agni Group started operations in 1991 as the authorized distributor of Mahindra utility vehicles and tractors. Over more than three decades, it has expanded from a vehicle importer into a nationwide automotive and mobility business with extensive sales and service infrastructure.

Our mission is to empower people, enrich lives, and contribute to Nepal's development through accessible mobility solutions. We claim a leadership position in Nepal's utility vehicle market, backed by a nationwide network of over 80 dealers.
